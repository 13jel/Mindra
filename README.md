Mindra

Mindra is a gentle daily state-tracking app designed to help you understand your mood, energy, and well-being over time. By logging small daily reflections, Mindra reveals meaningful personal patterns that support self-awareness, balance, and intentional living.

Features

Daily mood, energy, and well-being tracking

Simple, distraction-free logging experience

Pattern recognition through visual insights

Long-term trend monitoring

Lightweight and privacy-focused design

Purpose

Mindra was created to make self-reflection easy and sustainable. Instead of complex journaling, the app focuses on small daily inputs that gradually build a clear picture of your rhythms, helping you make better decisions about rest, work, and recovery.

Future Vision

Upcoming versions aim to include habit correlation insights, personalized pattern summaries, and gentle reminders that support a balanced daily rhythm.
